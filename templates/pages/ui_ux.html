Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our UI/UX design services

What is your design process?
Our process includes: Discovery & Research → Wireframing → Interactive Prototypes → Visual Design → Usability Testing → Developer Handoff.
How do you measure design success?
We track key metrics including task completion rate, time-on-task, user satisfaction scores (SUS), and conversion rates.
Do you provide design systems?
Yes, we create comprehensive design systems including component libraries, style guides, and developer documentation.
How do you ensure accessibility?
We follow WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines, use accessibility testing tools, and conduct testing with assistive technologies.
