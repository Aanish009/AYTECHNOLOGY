Our Story

AY Technology was born from a simple yet powerful idea: technology should empower businesses, not complicate them. Founded in 2016 by a group of passionate engineers and visionaries, we set out to bridge the gap between complex enterprise technology and accessible innovation.

What started as a small team of 5 developers in a co-working space has now grown into a global technology partner serving 250+ enterprises across 4 continents, with a 99% client satisfaction rate.
